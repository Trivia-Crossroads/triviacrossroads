Welcome!

Trivia Crossroads is a meeting-place for people who love good beer, good company, and crushing the spirits of their opponents with dazzling displays of erudition. Here you can find events in the Seattle area, look for just the right member to round out your team and increase your odds of trivia triumph, and make your own profile! When our minds meet, there's no one we can't beat!
